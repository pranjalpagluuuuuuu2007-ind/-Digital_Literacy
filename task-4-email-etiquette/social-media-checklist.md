# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Set your profiles to private to control who sees your content. | Don't post confidential information like your ID or home address. |
| Think twice before posting—if a recruiter saw it, would they hire you? | Don't engage in "flame wars" or cyberbullying in comment sections. |
| Fact-check information before sharing to avoid spreading "fake news." | Don't post photos of others without their explicit consent. |
| Use professional headshots for LinkedIn and networking sites. | Don't post while highly emotional or angry; wait 24 hours. |
| Use 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) to protect your accounts. | Don't click on suspicious links or "free gift" scams. |
